"Sound of Austin, TX" Implementing an IoT use case for Austin, creating a voice and plattform for Austin's artistic and technical creatives Interactive public art project collecting & selecting Austin's sounds and celebrating it's most unique sequence by broadcasting it daily .on people's devices during sunset. This is a usecase combining serverless IoT and blockchain technologies with machine learning and AI methodologies of gathering, sorting, distributing and creating consesus with React Native GUI . On a daily basis WATSON will decide which of the sound clips will be chosen for broadcasting the "Sound of Austin, TX" . All data collected and distributed uses public and private devices using AGORA.io that integrates seamlessly and allows userfriendly participation. http://SoundOfATX.com http://soundofatx.com Blockchain, REACT, AGORA
